ABOUT US

"Powering Indonesia's Future: Clean, Reliable, Sustainable with Sonus."

Tripower Solar Nusantara is a start-up enterprise that works in energy installation, precisely in the Engineering, Procurement, and Construction (EPC) of Solar Photovoltaic (PV) Panels, which we consider as a currently promising business in Indonesia.

Besides this business, Solar Nusantara also launches a program concerning with new renewable energy (NRE), which the program will provide NRE energy to the remote and isolated areas.

The NRE program involves a collaboration with relevant Village Enterprises (BUMDES) and local entrepreneurs, which the program will organize training sessions on EPC of Solar Photovoltaic Panels.
We call this program by a name: “PROGRAM INDONESIA TERANG”
